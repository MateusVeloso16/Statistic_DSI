Question 3 - Summarize Employee Engagement Score by Combinations of Gender
and Department using n, mean, standard deviation and coefficient of variation.

In[43]:
group_gd = df.groupby(["GENDER", "DEPT"])["EESCORE"]

summary_gd = group_gd.agg(
    n = "count",
    mean = "mean",
    sd = "std"
)

summary_gd["cv"] = summary_gd["sd"] / summary_gd["mean"]
